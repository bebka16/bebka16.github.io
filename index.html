History

Hi there. I've been developing Windows software since 2022. At first, I tried to make my own simple antivirus for one specific virus. It was an incredibly simple command-line program. Then the idea grew.
I started writing other programs — I made an archiver with encryption, built my own notepad with extended functionality, and then I moved into web development. I tried building a social network, a message board, cloud storage, and a secure anonymous messenger. I made my own analog of check-host.net, wrote browser extensions that warn about dangerous websites, and even published one in the Firefox Add-ons store.
All of this was brought together under one big roof — the Decibel Project — and everything was done within its framework. But over time, audience interest faded, and I completely shut down the old project. It was clunky, and its structure was hard to navigate.
Now I've decided to focus on specific projects that interest me the most, in order to make them useful for you. They're also now open source. Anyway, thanks for stopping by — I hope you enjoyed reading this. Of course, there was a lot of interesting stuff, but I'm too lazy to write it all here :)
